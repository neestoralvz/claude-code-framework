## Writing Instructions

### Direct
State action and target:
- Remove [X] from [Y]
- Change [element] to [desired]

### Specific
Provide exact context:
- In [location], change [item]
- Update [file]: replace [text]

### Pattern
Show desired format:
- Format as: 1. Item
           - Subitem

### Complete
Give full specification:
- [Action] [target]: [change] from [old] to [new]
